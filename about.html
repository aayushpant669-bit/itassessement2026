About Us

Founded in 2024, Brew It Café serves handcrafted coffee, fresh pastries and warm conversations.

Our mission is to create a relaxing and peaceful place for students, workers and dreamers.Welcome to Brew It Café, where every cup tells a story. Founded in 2024 by Aayush Pant, our cozy corner was born from a desire to bring artisanal, locally-roasted coffee and authentic pastries to the heart of Kathmandu. What started as a dream to create a warm, welcoming community space has blossomed into a beloved local spot where neighbors become friends.

We believe in more than just coffee—we believe in quality, sustainability, and connection. Our commitment to serving the best means: Locally Sourced: We partner with local farmers and artisans to ensure fresh, high-quality ingredients. Artisanal Quality: Every latte is handcrafted with precision and care by our passionate baristas. Community First: We strive to create a comfortable, inclusive environment where you can work, meet, or simply relax.

Meet the Team Behind every great cup is a great team! Come meet our friendly staff who are dedicated to making your day a little brighter with a welcoming smile and a perfectly brewed beverage.
